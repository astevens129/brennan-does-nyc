To See & Do

Want to walk the ley lines or find a little magic between subway stops? This is your to-do list for experiencing Brennan's NYC.

  • First things first: Brennan tells us to, you MUST ride the subway in order to
    "Confront your shared humanit."

      You have a few options:

    1. The digital tap and go option (which will cap your weekly fare at $34 per 7 day period-just ensure you use the same card for all of your trips) You may purchase an OMNY card if you don't have another tap card or don't have a digital wallet.
    2. Purchase a MetroCard where you can buy a 7 or 30 day unlimited pass with the option to add Express Buses or opt to pay $2.90 per ride.
    3. Buy an individual ride ticket for $3.25 per trip.
