— Brennan Lee Mulligan

🎧 More from Brennan

“The city is absolutely a beacon for people to come and be in the city of dreams, like in a very real way there is a big part of the New York story which is people who for one reason or another can’t be themselves in wherever they are, and the city affords them that.”
— Brennan Lee Mulligan, Brennan Lee Mulligan Talks D&D, NYC, and DADs
“…If you’re going to visit a place, go in with that Tony Bourdain attitude of like respectfulness, of like not treating it like a tourist experience. Of being like ‘I am here to humble myself in a place where I am not a native and I am going to come and try to really embrace the culture here as respectfully as possible.’ But also, definitely the part of like yeah there’s so much of New York that its economy thrives on tourism. We need people to come and go see Broadway and do all of the things here.”
— Brennan Lee Mulligan, Brennan Lee Mulligan Talks D&D, NYC, and DADs
“The best way to go to New York, I feel like, would be to stay with a friend or stay with someone who has a routine that they can impart on you. There is so much beauty in the city in just neighborhoods that are not going to have a landmark for you to go see, and for you to go support those smaller businesses and support those other places that are really like from native New Yorkers.”
— Brennan Lee Mulligan, Brennan Lee Mulligan Talks D&D, NYC, and DADs
